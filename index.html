Sam Maule

I graduated in Politics, Philosophy and Economics from the University of Warwick in 2012 and spent the 2010/11 academic year at UC Berkeley.

I worked at the Bank of England 2012-2015 analysing a range of financial markets from the perspective of monetary and finanical policymaking.

I then worked at climate think tank E3G working on the overlap between climate change and EU financial policymaking.

I went back to university 2017-18 to study for an MSc in Data Science at UCL, following a Graduate Certificate in Mathematics at Birkbeck.My MSc thesis predicted crop growth rates using mechanistic crop models and machine learning techniques for a Netherlands based company.
